Tool Warnings

If any of these things occur, take precaution and get a lab director:

Dangerous Signs

  • Material catches fire
    • Turn off machine, and put out fire (Or take material outside)
  • Loud, uncommon noises occur
    • Turn off machine, something may be caught or broken
  • Toxic smell appears
    • Stop the cut and leave the lid closed

This piece of equipment is always to be supervised by a Lab Assistant while being used

Equipment Calibration

  • Baseline Settings:
    • Do a test cut
    • If cut doesn't go through then increase force
    • Speed: 110
    • Force: Vinyl 120 | Heat Transfer 180
  • Bed Leveling:
    • Insert lab 3D printer SD card
    • Select print from media
    • Select "Level No Print"
    • Once leveled, repeat and select "Level with print"
    • Ask a lab director to teach you proper leveling if first time
  • Replace Basic Part
